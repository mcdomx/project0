ABOUT
A brief history

A lifelong interest in computing technology led me to leave my career in corporate finance to pursue a degree in computer engineering. At 12 years old, I started with a Commodore computer learning basic and spending evenings and weekends copying code from books to make games. Upon chosing a university degree, I opted to pursue Accounting as it seemed to offer greater opportunity at the time. I spent most of my career applying computing technology to financial processes and spent several years of my career focused on the mangement of information technology before working my way to a corporate controller position.

Over the years, I had the opportunity to pursue sevral part-time entrepreneurial interests while advancing my professional career. Directly out of university, I spent some of my free time doing basic accounting work for 2 small proprietorships. My career job eventually forced me to stop providing this service. Later, I recognized an underserved market in roommate placement services. I founded Roommate Finders with a weekly ad in the classified and second phone line. I matched hundreds of roommates over a 2 year period but had to close the business when my job took me to a new location overseas. After returning to the States, I found another underserved market in the area I was living in a purchased a PortraitEFX franchise. I spent 2 years as the team photographer for various sports leagues in the Pittsburgh area. Again, my career moved me and I had to reluctanly sell a very successful part-time business.

Outside of working, I've been able to pursue several personal endeavors including private piloting, bicycle racing and photography.

I aim combine my experience and skills in a future career in sofware engineering, design and management.
